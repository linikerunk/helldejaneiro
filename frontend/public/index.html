About the Game

The Story

In the heart of Rio de Janeiro, where samba rhythms collide with ancient gothic prophecies, a new world emerges. The city transforms at night, revealing a hidden realm where dragons soar above favelas and neon-lit streets hide portals to forgotten dimensions.

The Gameplay

Navigate through a grid-based world where every tile holds secrets. Combine the vibrant energy of Rio's culture with dark mystical powers. Dance with danger, forge alliances, and uncover the truth behind the Dark Harmony.

The Experience

Immerse yourself in a unique fusion of Brazilian carnival aesthetics and gothic fantasy. Every choice matters, every tile tells a story, and every moment pulses with electric energy of a city that never sleeps.
